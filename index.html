Book One

KEPT

A Captive Forced Proximity Dark Romance

He cooks me eggs. He watches me sleep. He has six loaded guns and he's expecting company.

I woke up in a stranger's cabin with no phone, no car, and the news that my sister is dead. The man who took me says he's protecting me. He says the people who killed her are coming. He says I'm next.

His name isn't Silas. I know that much. His body is scarred and his hands are steady enough to kill, gentle enough to cup the back of my skull like I'm the only breakable thing he's ever held.

He loved a woman who looked like me. That woman is gone.

I should run. Every door is unlocked. Every window opens.

I stay.

That should terrify me more than the men in the trees.

Book Two

FOUND

An Enemies-to-Lovers Dark Romantic Suspense

Three weeks. No call. No text. Her camera bag still on the kitchen table. Petra didn't pack. She didn't plan. She just disappeared.

Sloane Voss is a forensic scientist who reads crime scenes the way other people read body language. She's spent her career finding what powerful people want to stay lost.

Now she's looking for her sister. And someone has sent a man to make sure she stops looking.

Elias Vance is an operative. Trained, lethal, precise. Eleven days of surveillance. He knows her coffee order. Her walking route. The way her hands move when she's building a theory.

He was sent to stop her.

Instead, he pulls her into the dark with him.

Book Three

RUINED

A Possessive Hero Military Dark Romance

Six months. That's how long the quiet lasted.

We built a life. Portland, a house with good sightlines, morning coffee, the specific silence of two people who stopped running. I thought we earned this. I thought the worst was behind us.

Then Elias found the name. Victor Langford. Former Deputy Director of the Defense Intelligence Agency. The man who pulled the strings while seven soldiers died.

Langford is destroying evidence. Activating military contractors. And he is coming for my sister.

I will not lose Petra again.
